Party Trays

Planning on hosting a party and need food catered? Look no further, we would be happy to help make your life easier by offering party sized trays of our most popular dishes. Come on in or give us a call and we would be more than happy to help create a fantastic meal for you to serve at your party.

(562) 431-6496

NOTE: Please place your orders 48 hours in advance of your party to ensure the completion of your order.
Our party trays are for pick up only (no delivery). We apologize if this makes it and inconvenience but we appreciate your understanding.
Utensils and napkins are provided upon request.
To complete your order over the phone all we ask for is your name, a number we can reach you at, and your credit card number just to put on hold.
